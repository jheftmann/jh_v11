I help designers, design teams, and design-led organizations succeed through fractional creative leadership and coaching.

I'm a surfer and self-taught graphic designer. Originally from San Diego, I've spent most of my adult life between New York City and Europe, and I recently relocated to Los Angeles. I'm currently the Graphic Design Director at Grilli Type. In addition to my studio work, I teach, run workshops, give talks on topics related to design and technology, and work closely with recent graduates, creative leaders, founders, and and investors in mentorship and advisory roles.

Over 12 years of starting, scaling, and ultimately selling a design company, I learned everything from the basics of running a studio to the complexities of building a business with purpose. I received great feedback from my team, and developed not only a strong perspective on design, but also on leadership, company culture, and what it means to build a team aligned with a mission. The challenges were many—but the hardest, by far, was building a business that could run without me.
